Boreal Space wants to make low earth orbit available to all you DIY types - especially those with an interest in programming and tinkering with devices that YOU control. Working with Adafruit Industries, we have developed a Rapberry Pi processor board that we need to flight quality in order to launch it on an inexpensive CubeSat. Along with the ability to program the board, and join in with the elite group of coders we like to call the "500 mile hight club", we allow you to take control of our Lego Mindstorms robot, and carry out moves that are captured with our on-board video camera. Your programming skills will reach new highs (literally), and provide really, really cool bragging rights.

Space belongs to everyone, and by contributing to this project you help us launch another inexpensive but highly capable processor into low earth orbit. Having a Raspberry Pi at your command, you check out your code and become a part of humanity that is crowdsourcing the next phase of space exploration, what is commonly referred to as Space 2.0. Once you have the basics of programming Flight Software the sky is NOT the limit. You are limited only by your imagination. Heck, you can create your own space program. Create games, news and sports channels, broadcast a Valentine's Day message. Think Big!!!

FAQ

What's a CubeSat?
A CubeSat is an small, inexpensive satellite that democratizes access to space.
When will you launch the CubeSat?
We are currently scheduled to launch on August 8th, 2014.
